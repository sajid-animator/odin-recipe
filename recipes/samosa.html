SAMOSA

    DESCRIPTION

Good to see you here dear reader.Today I will share with you a great recipe of indian food which is SAMOSA.

You can make at home easily. Please follow all instructions carefully to make a good taste.

    INGREDIENTS

  • BOILED POTATOES
  • MAIDA FLOUR DOUGH
  • WHITE CHANNA
  • ONION
  • OIL

    RECIPE

  1. BOIL POTATOES AND MASH THEM WELL.
  2. MAKE MAIDA DOUGH MIXED WITH RED CHILLIES,CORIANDERS,CAROM SEEDS AND OIL.
  3. BOIL WHITE CHANNA AND MIX WITH MASHED POTATOES.
  4. CUT ONIONS AND MIX WITH POTATOES.
  5. MAKE BALLS OF DOUGH AND MAKE A ROUND SHAPED ROTI.
  6. CUT ROUND SHAPED ROTI IN 2 PARTS.
  7. Fold the edges of the semi-circle in order to give it a cone shape.
  8. Fill the semi-circle with potato filling and deep fry.
  9. Serve with tomato ketchup and green chutney. Enjoy it as a tea-time snack!
